THE 10 STEPS
================================================================================

DISCOVERY (Steps 1-4):
  1. List all processes                    ps, ps aux
  2. Filter processes by name              grep piping
  3. Parent/child relationships            PPID, pstree
  4. Monitor resources live                top, CPU, memory

CONTROL (Steps 5-8):
  5. First kill (graceful)                 kill, SIGTERM
  6. Understanding signals                 SIGTERM explained
  7. Last resort (force kill)              kill -9, SIGKILL
  8. Multiple processes & strategy         parent/child logic

APPLICATION (Steps 9-10):
  9. Real-world hunt                       troubleshooting
  10. Synthesis challenge                  capstone
